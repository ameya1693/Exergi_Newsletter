Exergi has received the prestigious AIS 156 (Amendment 3) Phase II certificate.

This prestigious certification highlights Exergi's commitment to maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and performance in its product offerings. The AIS 156 Amendment 3 Phase II certificate, issued by the Automotive Research Association of India (ARAI), is granted to companies that demonstrate exemplary compliance with rigorous safety requirements. This recognition places Exergi among the top-tier energy solutions providers in the industry.

As we continue to expand our carbon reduction footprint, the company plans to further invest in research and development to stay at the forefront of the rapidly evolving electric vehicle sector.

Exergi Launches Cutting-Edge LMFP Battery as Newest Addition to Product Lineup.

Exergi has announced the launch of its groundbreaking Lithium Manganese Fluorophosphate (LMFP) battery. This innovative new product is designed to provide higher energy density, increased safety, and longer cycle life compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries.

With its advanced technology, the LMFP battery offers several advantages over conventional lithium-ion batteries, including enhanced thermal stability, reduced risk of thermal runaway, and improved resistance to overcharging.
